SaaS has been a VC favourite sector for decades due to the consistency of impact, returns and scale of solutions.

## Types of SaaS companies globally


But with the emergence of LLMs - suppporting the idea of AGI - the potential to change the SaaS landscape seems endless. 

Let's compare how SaaS was used to look earlier:
- Clear problem statement 
- Complex web of solutions
- Repetative steps for every solution
- Experience improvement with depth in value prop
- Manpower to deduce the understanding of results attained

Now with AGI, we can envision SaaS as single interface to handle the complexity of the problem - the desired solution - the experience.
